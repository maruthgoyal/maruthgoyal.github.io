Maruth (MA-ruth) Goyal

About

I am a software engineer at Stripe on the Ruby Infrastructure team, based out of San Francisco. I recently graduated with a bachelors in Mathematics, and Computer Science in the Turing Scholars honors program at the University of Texas at Austin. I did research as a part of the UT Networked Systems lab advised by Aditya Akella. Recently I've been interested in all sorts of things ranging from databases, to cryptography, privacy-preserving algorithms, and program synthesis.

I enjoy playing the guitar a lot, and am a big fan of the Blues, and rock. I really like cooking (and eating) all sorts of food. I also quite like watching NBA. I grew up in Noida in India, but also spent time living in Hyderabad, Pune, and Mountain View, CA.
