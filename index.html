Maruth (MA-ruth) Goyal

About

I am a software engineer at Stripe on the Ruby Infrastructure team, based out of San Francisco. I graduated with bacherlors degrees in Mathematics, and Computer Science in the Turing Scholars honors program at the University of Texas at Austin. I did research as a part of the UT Networked Systems lab advised by Aditya Akella. I beleive realizing ubiquitous private, secure , and reliable compute is critical to the future of computation, and am interested in blending ideas from theoretical computer science and systems to help enable this.
